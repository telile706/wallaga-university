Welcome to Wallaga University

Established to serve the academic and developmental needs of Ethiopia, Wallaga University is committed to providing quality education, conducting impactful research, and engaging in community service. With multiple campuses across Oromia, we aim to be a center of excellence in teaching and innovation.

Mission: To produce competent graduates through practical and quality education.
Vision: To be one of the top research-oriented universities in East Africa by 2030.

Nekemte Campus

Our main campus in Nekemte is the administrative and academic center of the university. It hosts advanced laboratories, modern lecture halls, a digital library, and a 24/7 health facility. Students here enjoy a peaceful and scenic environment conducive to learning and research.

Shambu Campus

Located in Horo Guduru Wollega, Shambu Campus specializes in agriculture and environmental sciences. With its favorable climate and agricultural potential, it provides hands-on learning for students in crop production, animal sciences, and sustainable development.

Gimbi Campus

Gimbi Campus focuses on humanities, business, and natural sciences. It plays a key role in educating future leaders and professionals in the western region. The campus encourages community engagement and entrepreneurship through student-led initiatives.
